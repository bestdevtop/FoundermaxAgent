NovaMart Customer Support FAQ
Version 2026

=== SHIPPING TIMES ===

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days within the continental United States.
Express Shipping: 2-3 business days. Available at checkout for an additional fee.
Same-Day Delivery: Available in select metro areas for orders placed before 12 PM local time.

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days before shipment.
You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

=== CARRIERS ===

NovaMart uses NovaMart Logistics for most domestic shipments.
Express orders may be handed off to FedEx or UPS depending on your location.
International orders are shipped via DHL Express.

=== ADDRESS CHANGES ===

Before Shipment: You can change your shipping address from your account order page
or by contacting support before the order status changes to "Shipped".

After Shipment: Once an order has shipped, address changes cannot be made.
Contact the carrier directly using your tracking number, or refuse delivery
and contact us to reship once the package is returned.

=== INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING ===

NovaMart ships to over 40 countries. International orders may take 10-21 business days.
Customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes.
Some products (electronics with lithium batteries, certain food items) cannot be shipped internationally.
Digital products and gift cards are available worldwide with no shipping required.

=== PAYMENT METHODS ===

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
Gift cards and store credit can be applied at checkout.
Payment is charged when your order is placed, not when it ships.
For orders over $500, additional verification may be required.

=== ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT ===

Create an account at novamart.com to track orders, save addresses, and view order history.
You can reset your password via the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
To update your email address, go to Account Settings or contact support with ID verification.
To delete your account, contact support — this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

=== ORDER CANCELLATIONS ===

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement if they have not entered processing.
Once processing begins, cancellation is not guaranteed — contact support immediately.
Cancelled orders are refunded to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

=== CONTACT SUPPORT ===

Live chat: Available 24/7 on novamart.com
Email: support@novamart.com (response within 24 hours)
Phone: 1-800-NOVA-MART (Mon-Fri 8 AM - 8 PM ET)
