Refund & Return Policy v2026

1. Standard Return Window

Customers may request a refund within 30 calendar days of delivery.

Requests submitted after 30 days are automatically denied.

2. Eligible Refund Conditions

A refund may be approved when:

- The item arrived damaged.
- The item is defective.
- The wrong item was shipped.
- The package was lost in transit.
- The customer received fewer items than ordered.
- The item is unused and returned within the return window.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

- Final Sale products
- Clearance items
- Gift cards
- Digital downloads
- Subscription plans
- Personalized/customized products
- Hygiene products once opened
- Downloaded software licenses

These requests must be automatically denied.

4. Return Condition Requirements

Returned products must:

- Be in original condition
- Include original packaging
- Include accessories/manuals
- Show no signs of misuse

Failure to meet these requirements may result in:

- Partial refund (up to 50%)
- Refund denial

5. Refund Amount Rules

Order Value              | Action
-------------------------|----------------------------------
$0 - $500                | AI may approve/deny
$500.01 - $1,000         | Human review required
Above $1,000             | Senior manager approval required

6. Fraud Detection Rules

Refund requests are automatically escalated if:

- Customer has more than 3 refunds in the last 12 months.
- Customer claims "item not received" more than twice.
- Shipping address changed after order shipment.
- Customer account age is less than 30 days and refund exceeds $200.
- Refund amount exceeds $500.

7. Shipping Rules

Customer pays return shipping unless:

- Wrong item shipped
- Defective product
- Damaged in transit

In these cases, NovaMart provides a prepaid label.

8. Damaged Product Claims

Customers must provide:

- Minimum 2 photos
- Claim within 7 days of delivery

Otherwise, claim is denied.

9. Lost Package Claims

A lost package refund may be approved only when:

- Carrier tracking shows "lost"
- Delivery not confirmed
- 10 business days have passed

10. Exchange Rules

Customers may request an exchange instead of a refund.

Exchanges are allowed only within the 30-day return window.

11. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed:

- Credit Card: 5–10 business days
- PayPal: 3–5 business days
- Store Credit: Immediate
